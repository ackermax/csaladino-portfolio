About



Hello, and welcome to my site! My name is Carleen Saladino, and I am the photographer. I was born and raised in the Las Vegas Valley. I am a graduate of the College of Southern Nevada, and a current student at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. I have done photography work since 2011. Prior to my photography career, I was a substitute teacher for the Clark County School District.


I love working with families and children. Nothing brings me more joy than capturing the special moments in the lives of the families I photograph. It is my mission to create a fun and stress free photography experience for all of my clients.


If you are interested in booking a session with CSaladino Photography please feel free to email me at carlie@csaladino.com. I would love to work with you!

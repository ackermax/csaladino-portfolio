Photography Services



FAMILY SESSION: Family photo shoots with CSaladino Photography last a minimum of 1 hour at the location of your choice. Within this time we will work together to capture memories that you will cherish for a lifetime. While I will capture posed photographs, I also love to capture candid shots that truly show how your family interacts. I can’t wait to work with you! Please contact me for pricing and to book your session!


ENGAGEMENT & COUPLE SESSIONS: Engagement & Couple photo shoots will last a minimum of 1 hour. Engagement and couple shoots have a special place in my heart. I love being able to take a photo that captures the love shared between two people. Sessions will take place at the location(s) of your choice. I will work with you ahead of time to pick a location that will best capture your personality as a couple. Please feel free to contact me about pricing and to book your session!


WEDDINGS: CSaladino Photography will work with you to create the perfect Wedding Photography package. You may customize your wedding package to fit your specific needs. Some wedding services I provide are: Ceremony Candids, Reception Candids, Before Wedding Candids, Posed Wedding Party Portraits, and Separate Day Bridal’s (with or without groom). It is CSaladino Photography’s mission to help create beautiful images you will cherish for the rest of your lives together. I encourage you to contact me so we can discuss which services will best capture your special day.


MATERNITY SESSION: Maternity photo shoots last a minimum of 1 hour. Pregnancy is such a beautiful time. It is my job to help you capture this exciting time in your family’s life. Maternity sessions can take place at an outdoor location of your choice or inside your home. We will work together to decide on wardrobe and location ahead of time. The best time to photograph maternity pictures is between 32 to 36 weeks. So it is important to book your session well in advance so you are able to secure your date. Please contact me about pricing and to book your session.


NEWBORN SESSION: Newborn Photo shoots will last a minimum of two hours. Since I am a natural light photographer I do not offer studio portrait sessions. Instead I use the natural light found within the comfort of your own home to capture your little bundle of joy. I think it is important that Mom and Dad get in front of the camera so, we will take several full family shots within the session as well. Contact me for pricing and to book your session. When you book a Newborn Photo shoot with CSaladino Photography you will receive 10% off a Family shoot for your child’s first birthday.


HEAD SHOTS: Professional Headshot photo shoots will last a minimum of a 1/2 hour. You will choose the location and we will work together to create shots that meet your specific needs. Please contact me for pricing and available session dates.


BIRTHDAYS/SMALL EVENTS: One of the worst feelings after hosting a party or event is realizing after it’s over, you didn’t take any pictures of your special day! That’s where CSaladino Photography comes in. We will capture the festivities allowing you to relax and enjoy the day. Birthdays, Quinceañeras, Graduations, Retirement Parties, you name it! We will photograph any special event coming up in your life. So please contact me today for prices and to book your date!
